How can Goldbarb Educational Consulting help you?

It’s a tumultuous time in education — new standards, new assessments, new teaching demands, and new expectations for student performance. All of these factors make it difficult for educators to focus their efforts, but GEC shines a light on these challenges by helping...

  • - Clearly define educational goals;​
  • - Better understand what the new educational expectations mean for both teachers and students;
  • - Evaluate what role technology can play in the educational process;
  • - Provide insight-and tools to meet those challenges;
  • - Provide ‘just in time’professional development as you layout out, and implement your instructional plans; and
  • - Define a clear path to fill those gaps, and to improve instruction and student performance
